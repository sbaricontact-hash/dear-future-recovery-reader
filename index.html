Dear Future · Recovery Reader

Recover your preserved message safely

This standalone reader helps you recover a Dear Future preserved message using your recovery kit and recovery key. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Select the recovery kit text file you downloaded from your private receipt page. The reader will try to fill in the recovery details for you.

If you prefer, you can also open your recovery kit file and paste its preserved record section here. The reader accepts either the full recovery kit text or just the preserved record block.

Paste the recovery key exactly as shown in your recovery kit. It usually starts with dfk_v1_.
