Dear Future · Recovery Reader

Recover your preserved message locally

This standalone reader helps you decrypt a Dear Future preserved message or letter entirely in your browser. It does not depend on the Dear Future app being online.

Open your Dear Future Recovery Kit file and copy the JSON between START_PRESERVED_PAYLOAD_JSON and END_PRESERVED_PAYLOAD_JSON into this box.

Paste the recovery key exactly as shown in your recovery kit file. It usually starts with dfk_v1_.
